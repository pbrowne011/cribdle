About Cribdle

Cribdle is a cribbage game based on Wordle. Players aim to maximize their expected point total by selecting the best cards to discard.

How to Play

  1. You'll be given 6 cards by your opponent.
  2. Choose 2 cards to discard that make the best hand.
  3. When you submit, check your feedback.
    • Red: both the cards you selected were wrong
    • Yellow: one of the cards you selected was wrong. You don't know which one.
    • Green: both cards are correct. You win
