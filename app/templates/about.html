About Cribdle

Cribdle is a cribbage game based on Wordle. Players aim to maximize their expected value by selecting the best cards to discard.

How to Play

  1. You'll be given 6 cards in the first phase.
  2. Choose 2 cards to discard that maximize your expected value.
  3. In the second phase, you'll be given 5 cards and must discard 1.
  4. You have a total of 6 guesses for both phases combined.
